Tips On Taking care of a Kitten

Nutrition and Health

It is really important to feed your kitten with the proper food appropriate for their age and size. These meals should be balanced to ensure they are getting the right nutrients. Always consult with a veterinarian to advise on the right food for your kitten as well vaccination.

Litter Training

Litter training should be started as soon as possible to avoid messing everywhere. I trained the kitten by placing her in a litter basket after eating or waking up from a nap. This will make them get used to the routine however, consistency and patience is quite key here!

PlayTime

Try to provide as many toys as possible especially for a solo kitten. This is helpful for their playful nature even when you are not able to play with them. This is also good for their physical and mental development. Caution when choosing the right toys to avoid very small toys with sharp edges that may cause harm. Interactive toys may be a good option.
